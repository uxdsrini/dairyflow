DairyFlow Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

DairyFlow helps dairy businesses manage customers, milk subscriptions, deliveries, workers, attendance, billing, payments, and business reports. This Privacy Policy explains what data DairyFlow collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, mobile number, email address, and login identifiers.
  • Business data entered by users, including customer names, milk plans, orders, deliveries, billing records, payment records, worker details, attendance, salaries, and expense entries.
  • Subscription and payment information related to plan purchases and renewals processed through Razorpay.
  • Technical information such as device type, browser or app version, log data, and basic usage diagnostics used to improve reliability and security.

How We Use Information

  • To create and maintain user accounts.
  • To provide core DairyFlow features like billing, delivery management, profit tracking, and reporting.
  • To manage plan upgrades, trial access, payment confirmation, and subscription status.
  • To send operational notifications such as login alerts, payment reminders, or plan-related updates.
  • To improve app performance, prevent fraud, and troubleshoot technical issues.

Payments

DairyFlow uses Razorpay to process plan purchases. Payment credentials such as card, UPI, or bank details are handled by Razorpay and are not stored directly by DairyFlow, except for transaction references, payment status, and subscription metadata needed to activate plans and support users.

Data Storage and Security

DairyFlow stores application data using cloud services such as Firebase and related infrastructure. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect user data, but no platform can guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing

DairyFlow does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only:

  • With service providers needed to run the app, such as hosting, authentication, analytics, and payment partners.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or lawful government request.
  • To protect the safety, rights, or property of DairyFlow, users, or the public.

Data Retention

Account and business records are retained as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Users may request deletion of their account data, subject to operational and legal retention requirements.

User Choices

  • Users may update account details from within the app where supported.
  • Users may request deletion or correction of personal data by contacting support.
  • Users may stop using the service at any time, although some records may be retained where required.

Children’s Privacy

DairyFlow is intended for business use and is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so it can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.
