# DairyFlow Play Store Listing

## App name
`DairyFlow`

## Short description
`Manage dairy billing, deliveries, workers and profit in one app.`

## Full description
`DairyFlow` helps dairy owners run daily operations from one simple dashboard.

Manage customers, recurring milk subscriptions, one-time orders, route-wise deliveries, monthly billing, customer payments, staff attendance, salaries, expenses, and profit reports without juggling notebooks or spreadsheets.

### Why DairyFlow
- Track daily milk deliveries and customer plans
- Generate monthly bills and monitor collections
- Record expenses and understand profit clearly
- Manage workers, attendance, and salaries
- Access the app on mobile for daily field use

### Features
- Customer management
- Milk subscriptions
- Order management
- Billing and payment tracking
- Delivery tracking
- Worker and attendance management
- Salary management
- Expense tracking
- Revenue and profit reporting
- Premium analytics and advanced reporting

### Plans
- `Starter` for small dairy businesses
- `Growth` for growing operations with team management
- `Premium` for advanced reporting, multi-user access, and future AI features

### Trial and upgrades
Registered users receive a 30-day Premium trial. After the trial, DairyFlow shows available plans and activates paid access after Razorpay confirms payment.
